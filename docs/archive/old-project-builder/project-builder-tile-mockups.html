Hybrid Strategy

Use different approaches based on the nature of each step:

  • Step 1 (Project Type): Use full background photos (Option 2A)
  • Step 2 (Project Details): Use full background photos (Option 2A)
  • Step 3 (Complexity): Keep current icons
  • Step 4 (Budget): Keep current icons

Photo Requirements

  • Dimensions: 800x600px minimum (4:3 ratio)
  • Format: WebP with JPEG fallback
  • Quality: High-res, professional construction photos
  • Subject: Real projects, well-lit, clear focal point
  • Quantity needed: ~15-20 photos (3 for Step 1, 12+ for Step 2 variations)
