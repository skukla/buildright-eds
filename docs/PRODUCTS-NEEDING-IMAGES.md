## Image Generation Guidelines

### Product Photography Style
- Clean white or light gray background
- Professional product shot
- Multiple angles if helpful (front, side, detail)
- Show scale/dimensions visually when relevant
- Include packaging for boxed items (nails, screws)

### Dimensions & Format
- **Format**: PNG with transparency (preferred) or white background
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1200x1200px (for zoom capability)
- **Aspect Ratio**: Square (1:1) or product-appropriate
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (< 500KB per image)

### Naming Convention
- **Format**: `{SKU}.png`
- **Example**: `NAIL-244FD20C.png`
- **Location**: `buildright-eds/images/products/`
