## How to Find More Images

1. **Unsplash**: Go to https://unsplash.com and search for:
   - "2x4 lumber stud"
   - "plywood sheet"
   - "metal stud framing"
   - "drywall sheet"
   - Click on an image, then use the "Download" button to get the direct URL

2. **Pexels**: Go to https://www.pexels.com and search for:
   - "construction lumber"
   - "plywood texture"
   - "metal framing"
   - "drywall installation"
   - Click on an image, then copy the image URL

3. **Pixabay**: Go to https://pixabay.com and search for:
   - "building materials"
   - "lumber studs"
   - "construction materials"
